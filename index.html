Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is Yuvarohan?
Yuvarohan is a youth-focused fitness and sports development program designed to promote physical well-being, discipline, and athletic excellence among young individuals.
Q2. Who can join Yuvarohan?
Anyone between the ages of 10 and 25 who is interested in improving their fitness or pursuing sports professionally can join the Yuvarohan program.
Q3. Is prior sports experience required?
No, beginners are welcome. The program offers training modules tailored to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced athletes.
Q4. What kind of sports are included?
Yuvarohan includes a range of sports like athletics, football, basketball, martial arts, yoga, and more, depending on the local facilities.
Q5. Are there any fees to join?
Some programs are free, while others may require minimal fees for coaching, equipment, or facility use. Fee details depend on your location and chosen program.
Q6. How do I register?
You can register online via the official Yuvarohan portal or visit your nearest training center for offline registration.
